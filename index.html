About Me

Engr Enyinnaya Chima Nwafor is a devoted Christian and a knight of St, Christopher of the Anglican Communion. He belongs to many relevant professional bodies and reputable social organizations such as; Member Nigerian society of Engineers (NSE) Member , Council for the regulation of Engineering in Nigeria (COREN) Honorary fellow, Nigerian Environmental Society (FNES) Fellow, Chartered institute of project managers of Nigeria ( CIPMN) Platinum member, Aba Sports club Member, Ngwa Patriot Forum His interest in politics is based on his conviction that it will be a veritable tool and an instrument to drive development to the grassroots and deepen democracy in Nigeria for the benefit of all her citizenry.
