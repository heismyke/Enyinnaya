YPP PARTY

The Young Progressives Party (YPP) is a social-democratic political party in Nigeria founded in 2017.

HEADQUARTERS: Block 10, Flat No. 1 Benue Crescent, Area 1, Garki Abuja

CHAIRMAN: Bishop Amakiri

SECRETARY: Vidiyeno Bamaiyi

IDEOLOGY: Social democracy

COLORS: Yellow, red, green

SLOGAN: Service to the people

WEBSITE: ypp.ng

In its manifesto, it called for concerted efforts to improve welfare, fight for social justice and provide equality of opportunities for all citizens in Nigeria.

HISTORY; The Young Progressive Party was formed in 2016 as a political association. On 7 June 2017, the association was officially registered as a political party having met all the requirements of being a party in Nigeria. The party was formed on the principles of social democracy and inclusive governance and the party has over 30 million members across Nigeria.

Projects

In 2008, Engr Enyinnaya Nwafor founded, TUNNEL END INVESTMENT LIMITED. The philosophy behind Tunnel End was to run a construction Company with wide clientele so much that he could share profit from it with mankind. He is also the founder of Rock Waters Integrated Services Ltd., two construction companies headquartered in Aba. He has through his Non-Governmental Organization, “EACH ONE AID ONE FOUNDATION” lend a helping hand to our society at different times. The Foundation also embarks on free medical programmes, Poverty alleviation for the youths, men and women through various empowernment templates in it’s kitty like :- grants, distribution of vehicles, motorcycles, wrappers, building of houses, distribution of rice and fertilizers, and other food items. “Each One Aid One” has inadvertently become a beacon of hope for the depraved widows, indigent student, hopeless entrepreneur, the public servant grappling with poor welfare and some organizations battling with realities of harsh economic climate in the country. #abiarestoration2023 #nanamovement
