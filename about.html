Biography

Born on the 17th day of December, 1975 in Umuojima Ogbu, Osisioma Ngwa LGA, Abia State. He is the son of Late Dr Chima Nwafor, a two time Deputy Governor of Abia and Dame Gladys Nne Nwafor of Mbutu Umuojima Ogbu Village, Osisioma Ngwa LGA, Abia State. A graduate of Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Federal University of Technology Owerri (Futo). He has through his Non-Governmental Organization, “EACH ONE AID ONE FOUNDATION” lend a helping hand to our society at different times.
The Foundation also embarks on free medical programmes, Poverty alleviation for the youths, men and women through various empowernment templates in it’s kitty like :- grants, distribution of vehicles, motorcycles, wrappers, building of houses, distribution of rice and fertilizers, and other food items. “Each One Aid One” has inadvertently become a beacon of hope for the depraved widows, indigent student, hopeless entrepreneur, the public servant grappling with poor welfare and some organizations battling with realities of harsh economic climate in the country.

Projects

Projects

In 2008, Engr Enyinnaya Nwafor founded, TUNNEL END INVESTMENT LIMITED. The philosophy behind Tunnel End was to run a construction Company with wide clientele so much that he could share profit from it with mankind. He is also the founder of Rock Waters Integrated Services Ltd., two construction companies headquartered in Aba.
Engr Enyinnaya Nwafor is the man to beat in any transparent political process as he has overwhelming massive support from the grassroots – men, women and the youths across the 3 Senatorial zones. Here is a man that has the people at heart. Engr Sir Enyinnaya Chima Nwafor loves Abia and it’s his utmost wish that Abia should take it’s proper place in the comity of states in Nigeria.
